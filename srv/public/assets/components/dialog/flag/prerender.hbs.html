Flag Track as Not A Mashup

Mashup A mashup (also mesh, mash up, mash-up, blend, bootleg and bastard pop/rock) is a song or composition created by blending two or more pre-recorded songs, usually by overlaying the vocal track of one song seamlessly over the instrumental track of another.
A Mashup is not:
  • A remix of a single song
  • A DJ track mix where songs overlap
  • A medley of songs with similar styles
